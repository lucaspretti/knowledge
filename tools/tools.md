## Notes

- [Tips for success in building libraries/tools](https://youtu.be/oiNtnehlaTo?t=2861):
  1. Scratch your own itch.
  2. Stand on the shoulders of giants.
  3. Solve for the 80 % use case.
  4. Make it easy to adopt/delete.
